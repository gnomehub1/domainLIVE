Frequently Asked Questions
▶ How long does shipping take?

Rugs typically take 1-2 weeks to create and ship, depending on availability. We always try to deliver ASAP! Shipping within Colombia is FREE, and international shipping times vary by location.

▶ Can I customize my rug design?

Absolutely! Use our Contact form and upload any photo or image to discuss your vision. We will send you a FREE digital proposal within 24 hours.

▶ What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments through Mercado Pago (Latin America) and PayPal (International). Colombian customers can use any Colombian bank, including Nequi, simply by scanning our QR code or using our Bre-B key.

▶ How do I care for my rug?

Use a vacuum or wet wipes for regular cleaning. DO NOT use a washing machine or expose to direct sunlight. With proper care, your rug will last for years. All rugs include a 1-year warranty!

▶ What are international shipping costs?

Colombia enjoys FREE shipping! For international orders:

Gospel & Wall/Floor Rugs:
• Latin America, US & Canada: add $30 USD shipping fee
• Europe & Australia: add $80 USD shipping fee

Keyboard Rugs:
• Latin America, US & Canada: add $25 USD shipping fee
• Europe & Australia: add $50 USD shipping fee

▶ Do you offer refunds or exchanges?

We stand behind our quality! If there's an issue with your rug, contact us within 7 days of delivery. All rugs come with a 1-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. If you are not satisfied with the final result, ship us the Rug back and you'll get 100% of your money back, NO QUESTIONS ASKED! (Shipping fees NOT included)

▶ What makes the rugs special?

Every rug is a unique piece of art featuring bold colors and angular patterns. Each rug is hand-tufted with premium acrylic yarn, featuring non-slip backing and carved 3D details for texture and depth.

▶ Can I see a preview before ordering?

Yes! For custom designs, we provide a FREE digital mockup within 24 hours of your photo/image submission. Once you approve the design, we require a deposit to begin crafting your masterpiece. For Gospel, Keyboard, or Wall/Floor rugs, what you see in our gallery is what you get!
